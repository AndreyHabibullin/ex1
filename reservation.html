RESERVE BY PHONE

We take reservations for lunch and dinner. To make a reservation, please call us at 8 (908) 176 43-62 between 10am-6pm, Monday to Friday. We do not book the bar area – we leave this for walk-in guests to ensure that we always offer some tables for those who have not booked.

PRIVATE DINING & EVENTS

Whatever your event or celebration, we are here to make your special day a truly memorable one. Rostov’s private dining experts are more than happy to create custom floral arrangements and arrange for guests to meet Chef Moise during the course of their meal. For parties of 8-12 we have the roastery table which is available for private hire.
